1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the site to recognise your device, remember your preferences, and provide a more personalised experience.

We also use similar technologies such as web beacons, pixel tags, and local storage. References to "cookies" in this policy include these similar technologies.

🍪 By continuing to use the Inizio Global Platform, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can manage your preferences at any time via your browser settings.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Always Active

Essential for the Platform to function. These cookies enable core features like secure login, session management, and fraud prevention. They cannot be disabled.

Performance & Analytics Optional

Help us understand how users interact with the Platform — which pages are visited most, where errors occur, and how we can improve. Data is aggregated and anonymised.

Functional Optional

Remember your preferences such as language, display settings, and previously viewed products to provide a more personalised experience.

Marketing Optional

Used to deliver relevant advertising and measure campaign effectiveness. We do not use aggressive tracking or share data with ad networks without your consent.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use a limited number of trusted third-party services that may place cookies on your device:

  • Google Analytics — web analytics (opt-out available via Google's opt-out tool)
  • Intercom — customer support chat widget
  • Cloudflare — security and performance

These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies. We encourage you to review them.

5. Your Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block all or certain cookies
  • Set preferences for specific websites

Note that disabling necessary cookies will impair Platform functionality and may prevent you from logging in. For guidance on managing cookies in your specific browser, visit your browser's help documentation.

Opt-Out Links

  • Google Analytics: tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout
  • Intercom: docs.intercom.com/privacy
