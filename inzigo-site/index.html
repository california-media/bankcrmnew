Everything you need to know about Inizio Global and how the platform works.

Inizio Global is a financial distribution platform that connects financial institutions, agencies, and independent sales professionals to help customers access a range of financial products through a single ecosystem.
No. Inizio Global is not a bank and does not provide financial products directly. All products are offered, reviewed, and approved by participating financial institutions.
Inizio Global welcomes independent sales professionals, banking consultants, agencies, and financial institutions looking to grow their distribution network and business opportunities.
Depending on partner availability, products may include credit cards, personal loans, auto finance, business finance, bank accounts, and investment solutions.
Sales professionals and agencies submit customer applications through the platform. Partner financial institutions review applications and make approval decisions based on their own criteria and policies.
Agents earn commissions when customers they refer successfully complete the approval and activation process for eligible financial products offered by partner institutions.
Commissions are typically paid after a product has been approved and activated. Payment schedules and commission rates vary by financial institution, product, and partnership agreement.
